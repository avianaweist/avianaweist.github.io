HOW TO ADD YOUR HEADSHOT
=========================

The portfolio site looks for a photo at:

  images/avi-headshot.jpg

Just drop a photo into this "images" folder and name it exactly
"avi-headshot.jpg" (or edit the filename in the <img src="..."> tag
in the hero section of avianaweist_portfolio.html to match whatever
file you use).

Until a real photo is added, the hero section automatically shows a
clean navy "AW" monogram instead - so the site looks finished either
way. No other changes needed once the file is in place; just refresh
the page.

Recommended photo specs: a vertical/portrait-oriented shot, at least
600x720px, well-lit, professional but approachable (this is the shot
that'll sit right next to your name at the top of the site).
